Are you a parent or children education worker who needs to measure a child's height? As parents ourselves we know how difficult this can be specially when you do not have a fancy doctor's office scale. The purpose behind Mobile Height App is to give you the tool necessary to easily keep track a child's growth. Because children have many times more energy than adult does they will not stand still for more than a few seconds. This is where Mobile Height comes in.

Its simple point-and-shoot design style is meant to allow you to capture that height measurement as quickly and simply as possible. Also, because it does not require rulers or scales hanging from a wall to compute height you can work on the go. No more having to line a child against a wall or chasing them around the room because the cannot stop playing to go and get measured. only a business card sized object is enough for Mobile Height to set a frame of reference and show you the data.

FAQ

How does Mobile Height work?
By using proven computer vision techniques Mobile Height works out the distance from your mobile device to the ground.
On what platforms is Mobile Height available?
Mobile Height is available for Android and iOs.
